For new users: Click Remind Me Later (or Dismiss if you don't 
want to se this message again), another message should pop up.
Click FRESH INSTALL! Once install is done, click force close
and re-open kodi afterwards and you're good to go.
For old users: Go to update to check if new version has arived.
(also you will get a little auto notis when new update is available)
If you have made some custom changes; chose Standard install.
Otherwise chose Fresh Install.

First Time Users! (authorize Trakt)
You will be asked to authorize trakt from both Track and Elementum.
First if you dont have a trakt account.. go to trakt.tv and make one now!
You will also need to go in to one movie then go to settings
and choose OpenMeta There you will go to Trakt and chose authorize.
When you have authorized ALL 3 addons you are good to go.
(all your settings will be saved later if and when you update yoyr system)
!!!IF YOU ALLREADY HAVE A TRAKT ACCOUNT!!! (!First Time Users ONLY!) 
Then you have to make shure to authorize Trakt Addon LAST!!!
(or else you risk losing your whole collection) YIKES!

First Time Users! (Enable VPN)
Go to Addons -> My Addons -> Programs -> VPN manager for openvpn
And Enable the addon. a pop up will apear and ask you a question.
Chose Wizard, and put in your credentials and follow the wizard.
